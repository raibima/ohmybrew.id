# Why Oh My Brew exists

Oh My Brew started with a simple idea: **good coffee should feel welcoming**.

We love coffee that tastes clean, sweet, and interesting, but we do not want the experience to feel intimidating. We want the brand to feel warm, a little playful, and easy to come back to, whether you are ordering your usual latte or trying a fruitier black coffee for the first time.

## What we are serving first

Right now, we are starting with the drinks people already love to order online:

- creamy espresso-based drinks
- stronger milk coffees with a clearer coffee finish
- a few black coffee options with brighter flavors

That gives us a practical way to introduce the shop while we keep nudging people toward **fruity** and **floral** espresso and black coffee profiles.

## Why online first

We are launching as an **online-only** coffee shop in BSD, Tangerang.

That lets us stay focused on what matters most early on:

1. making drinks consistently
2. keeping prices accessible
3. learning what customers come back for

We are currently taking orders through delivery platforms, and the goal is to make every cup feel like it came from a neighborhood cafe with a bit more personality.

> Good coffee can be serious about quality without acting serious all the time.
